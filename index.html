What is Tehfīz?

Built for teachers who care about the details

Hifz teaching has always relied on the teacher's memory and handwritten notes. Tehfīz replaces that with a fast, word-precise digital tool — so teachers can focus on teaching, and students can review their sessions with full clarity.

The teacher logs mistakes word by word on the actual Quran text and records how much help was needed at each point. The student receives a session file, opens it in the Viewer, and sees every mistake highlighted exactly where it occurred — with the intervention level and teacher notes attached.

How it works

Simple for teachers. Clear for students.

1
📖
Teacher opens the Logger

During or after a recitation session, the teacher opens the Feedback Logger on their phone or computer — no installation needed.

2
✏️
Mistakes are logged word by word

The teacher taps any Arabic word to log a mistake, selects the type (Hesitation, Wrong word, Makhraj, Tajweed, etc.) and records the intervention level (0–3).

3
📤
Session file is shared

The teacher exports a small .qrs file and sends it to the student via WhatsApp, email, or any messenger.

4
📱
Student opens the Viewer

The student imports the file into the free Feedback Viewer and sees every mistake highlighted on the Quran text — with intervention level and notes visible.

5
📊
Review and reflect

Tap any highlighted word to read teacher notes. Charts break down mistake types and intervention levels so students know where to focus revision.

6
🔄
Track progress over time

Multiple sessions can be loaded together to compare improvement and identify persistent weak points across many recitations.
