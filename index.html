What is Tehfīz?

Built for teachers who care about the details

Hifz teaching has always relied on the teacher's memory and handwritten notes to track a student's mistakes. Tehfīz replaces that with a fast, word-precise digital tool — so teachers can focus on teaching, and students can review their sessions with full clarity.

The teacher marks mistakes word by word on the actual Quran text during recitation. The student receives a session file, opens it in the Viewer app, and sees every mistake highlighted exactly where it occurred — with the teacher's notes attached.

How it works

Simple for teachers. Clear for students.

1
📖
Teacher opens the Tracker

During recitation, the teacher opens the Tracker app on their phone or computer — no installation needed.

2
✏️
Mistakes are marked word by word

The teacher taps any Arabic word to log a mistake, selects the type and how it was corrected, and adds optional notes.

3
📤
Session file is shared

The teacher exports a small .qrs file and sends it to the student via WhatsApp, email, or any messenger.

4
📱
Student opens the Viewer

The student imports the file into the free Viewer app and sees every mistake highlighted on the actual Quran text.

5
📊
Review and reflect

The student can tap any highlighted word to read the teacher's notes, see charts, and review sessions over time.

6
🔄
Track progress

Multiple sessions can be loaded together to spot repeated mistakes and see improvement across recitations.
